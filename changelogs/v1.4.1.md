# Version 1.4.1
**Released:** April 4, 2026

## 🎉 What's New

- **Dynamic payment methods selector** — Agencies can now choose which Stripe payment methods clients can use when paying invoices. The selector shows all supported methods with real-time active/inactive status pulled directly from your Stripe account capabilities.
- **BNPL support** — Klarna, Afterpay/Clearpay, and Affirm are available for agencies whose Stripe accounts support them, with a direct link to enable them in the Stripe dashboard.
- **International payment methods** — SEPA Direct Debit, iDEAL, and Bacs Direct Debit are now selectable for agencies serving European and UK customers.
- **Stripe fee transparency** — Each payment method now shows its Stripe transaction fee so you can make informed decisions about what to offer clients.

## ✨ Improvements

- Payment methods section is collapsed by default to keep the settings page clean — click to expand.
- Inactive payment methods show clear explanations: BNPL methods link to the Stripe dashboard to enable, while other unavailable methods show a tooltip explaining the limitation.
- At least one payment method must remain selected at all times — the last selected method is locked to prevent accidentally disabling all checkout options.
- Card is pre-selected by default and Apple Pay / Google Pay are included automatically when card is enabled.
- Sessions now persist for 30 days, so you stay logged in across browser restarts instead of being signed out every session.

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

- Checkout sessions now fall back to card-only if a previously saved payment method is no longer available on your Stripe account, preventing failed payment links.
